Why Do I Need Credit Checks for my Essex Business?

‍

It’s exciting to bring a new client or contractor on board, with the opportunity to make more money and streamline existing processes. Whilst you focus on building profitable and personal rapport with new clients, we can efficiently and discreetly check the security of their financial position before you sign on the dotted line.

Outsourced Credit Control Essex will assign you a dedicated Account Manager who will carefully consider the company in question, evaluate their credit score and details; then summarise any potential area of concern to your business in a clear and digestible format.

 

Outsourced Credit Control can:

  • Provide full financial reports within just 24 hours
  • Advise you on the best next steps for your business to take regarding working with the company in question, or finding someone else.
  • If you decide to continue with the supplier or contractor, we can ensure your processes are firmly in place to mitigate implications to your business

Contact us on the form below or by booking a video call to see how we can help your Essex business's credit control.

Our Checks Include:

Notification of any outstanding CCJs
A suggested credit limit or advance payment basis
Advice on whether the client is known to delay payment
Background details including directors, turnover, profitability
