Late Payment Calculator

Under the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act, you have the right to charge your customers compensation and statutory interest on any overdue invoices to help cover debt collection costs.

The late payment interest is the extra amount of money that your customer will have to pay if they fail to pay the amount by the contracted date.

For a free calculation of how much late payment interest you can charge please download the calculator below.

Letter Before Action Template

A Letter Before Action (LBA) is a formal letter that requests the payment of the debt and warns of the imminent issue of a court claim.  An LBA sets out what is owed and provides a set time period in which to pay.  Before issuing legal proceedings, a Letter Before Action must be sent or costs may be forfeited.

To help you write a Letter Before Action we have provided a FREE LBA template here.  Feel free to amend with the details of your debt.  

We’ll be happy to provide any advice on this if needed.
